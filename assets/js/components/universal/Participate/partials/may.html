What are Treasury, Departments and Parliament busy with?

  • National Treasury issues budget guidelines to departments requesting budget submissions, including their 3-year expenditure estimates, in accordance with the adopted budget strategy and planned budget process.
  • Departments engage with analysts on departmental budgets and the drafting of budget submissions. (May & June)
  • Parliament and provincial legislatures review the provincial appropriation bill and each department's budget, including the department's Annual Performance Plan (APP) and Strategic Plan (SP).

How can I participate?

  • Review the departments' Annual Annual Performance Plans (APPs) and Strategic Plans (SPs).
  • Engage national departments and relevant Parliamentary committees on the content of the APPs and SPs and whether national departmental budgets have been properly prioritised.
  • Engage provincial departments and relevant provincial legislature committees on the content of the APPs and SPs and whether national departmental budgets have been properly prioritised.
