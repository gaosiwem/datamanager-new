What are Treasury, Departments and Parliament busy with?

  • Departments table strategic and annual performance plans.
  • Provincial budgets are tabled and submitted to National Treasury.
  • Within 16 days of the Budget being tabled, the Committees on Finance must submit a report to the National Assembly and the National Council of Provinces on the fiscal framework and revenue proposals.
  • The Committees on Finance hold public hearings when deliberating the contents and recommendations to be included in its reports.

How can I participate?

  • Make submissions to the Committees on Finance providing your input on the fiscal framework and the revenue (tax) proposals.
